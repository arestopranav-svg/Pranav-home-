Hello, Universe

Passionate About Everything Beyond The Sky

I'm Pranav, an astronomy content creator driven by a simple obsession: what's out there? From the rocky worlds of our solar system to galaxies billions of light-years away, I create content that makes the universe feel close, clear and endlessly fascinating.

My work dives into planets, galaxies, black holes, future technology and cosmic exploration — breaking down complex space science into visuals and stories that spark curiosity. Whether it's the latest telescope discovery or the physics of a dying star, there's always something new to explore.

Through PRANAV COSMIC & UNIVERSE EXPLORE, I'm building a community of stargazers who believe the greatest adventure is the one above us.
